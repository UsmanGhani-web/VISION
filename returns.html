Returns FAQ

How long do I have to return an item?

Most products can be returned within 14 days of delivery. Custom builds and special orders may have different return terms.

What if I receive a damaged product?

Contact us immediately and take photos of the damage. We'll arrange a free return and replacement or refund.

Can I return a custom-built PC?

Custom builds have a 7-day return window and may incur a 15% restocking fee. Contact us for specific terms.

How long does the refund process take?

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive and inspect your return.

Do I need the original receipt?

Yes, please include your order confirmation or receipt with your return for faster processing.

Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?

Yes, we offer exchanges for most products. You can choose a different size, color, or model of equal value.
