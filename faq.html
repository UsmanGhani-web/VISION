General Questions

What is beastro?

beastro is Pakistan's premier destination for premium gaming PC accessories and components. We specialize in high-quality gaming peripherals, custom PC builds, and expert technical support.

Where are you located?

We are based in Pakistan and serve customers nationwide. Our main office is located in Karachi, and we offer shipping to all major cities and towns across Pakistan.

What are your business hours?

Our customer support team is available Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM PKT. Online orders can be placed 24/7 through our website.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we focus on serving customers within Pakistan. However, we're working on expanding our international shipping capabilities in the near future.

Product Questions

Are your products genuine and authentic?

Yes, all our products are 100% genuine and purchased directly from authorized distributors and manufacturers. We never sell counterfeit or replica products.

Do you offer product customization?

Absolutely! We offer custom PC builds where you can choose every component according to your preferences and budget. We also provide RGB customization for gaming peripherals.

Can I test products before purchasing?

Yes, we have a showroom where customers can test our gaming peripherals and see our custom builds in action. Contact us to schedule a visit.

Do you have a warranty on all products?

Yes, all our products come with manufacturer warranty. Additionally, we provide extended warranty options for most products to give you extra peace of mind.

Shipping Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping within major cities takes 2-3 business days. For remote areas, it may take 5-7 business days. Express shipping is available for faster delivery.

What are your shipping rates?

Shipping rates vary by location and weight. Orders over PKR 10,000 qualify for free standard shipping. Express shipping costs PKR 500-1000 depending on location.

Do you ship to all cities in Pakistan?

Yes, we ship to all cities and towns across Pakistan. We use reliable courier services to ensure safe and timely delivery to even the most remote locations.

Can I track my order?

Yes, you'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. You can track your package in real-time through our website or the courier's tracking system.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We offer a 14-day return policy for most products. Products must be in original condition with all packaging and accessories. Some items like custom builds may have different return terms.

How do I return a product?

Contact our customer service team to initiate a return. We'll provide you with a return authorization number and shipping label. Returns are processed within 5-7 business days.

When will I receive my refund?

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after we receive and inspect your returned item. The refund will be issued to your original payment method.

Are return shipping costs covered?

We cover return shipping costs for defective products or shipping errors on our part. For customer preference returns, return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility.

Warranty & Support

What warranty do you provide?

All products come with manufacturer warranty (typically 1-3 years). We also offer extended warranty plans that can extend coverage up to 5 years for additional protection.

How do I claim warranty service?

Contact our warranty department with your purchase details and issue description. We'll guide you through the process and handle communication with manufacturers on your behalf.

Do you provide technical support?

Yes, we provide comprehensive technical support for all our products. Our team of experts can help with setup, troubleshooting, and optimization advice.

What's covered under warranty?

Warranty covers manufacturing defects and hardware failures under normal use. It does not cover physical damage, water damage, or issues caused by improper use or modifications.

Technical Support

Can you help me build a custom PC?

Absolutely! Our expert technicians can help you design and build a custom PC tailored to your needs and budget. We offer consultation, parts selection, and professional assembly services.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes, we offer professional installation services for all our products. This includes PC assembly, peripheral setup, software installation, and system optimization.

Can you help with gaming setup optimization?

Yes! Our gaming experts can help optimize your setup for maximum performance, including graphics settings, peripheral configuration, and system tweaks for better gaming experience.

Do you provide remote support?

Yes, we offer remote technical support for software issues and basic troubleshooting. For hardware problems, we may need to see the device in person or have it shipped to our service center.
