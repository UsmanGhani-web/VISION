Warranty FAQ

How long is the warranty valid?

Standard warranty is 1-3 years depending on the product. Custom builds come with 2-5 years, and extended plans can add up to 3 additional years.

Can I transfer warranty to another person?

Yes, warranties are transferable to new owners. Contact us with the transfer details and we'll update the warranty registration.

What if I can't find my receipt?

We can look up your purchase using your name, phone number, or email address. However, having the receipt speeds up the process.

Do you offer international warranty?

Currently, our warranty services are limited to Pakistan. We're working on expanding international coverage in the future.

Can I upgrade my warranty plan?

Yes, you can upgrade to a higher warranty plan at any time. The new plan will extend your coverage from the current date.

What happens if a part is discontinued?

If a part is no longer available, we'll replace it with an equivalent or better model at no additional cost to you.
